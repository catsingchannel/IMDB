"IMDB" : Infectious diseases pathogens' Mutation profile Database

"IMDB" is an interactive web-based platform and set of analytic tools for effcient analysis of mutations among thousands of respiratory infectious diseases samples and providing in-silico applications based on mutation profiles such as automatical qPCR assays design;

Protein Mutation Profile

This profile is for the top mutated events occurred in proteins encoded by virus genome. These events may be considered as potential targets for clinical therapy, as some mutations are critical for virus infection and transmission. (e.g. D614G for SARS-CoV-2).
